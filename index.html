We are a phone repair company based out of beautiful Victoria, British Columbia. We specialize in repairing cracked or non-functioning phones, tablets, or even phablet screens.

Our team consists of university students who know your phone better than their calculus textbook. The team has 4+ years of indepth experience with phones, computers, and other technology. They come from completely different areas of technology, which makes them a technologically diverse, knowledgeable, dream team.

VicFixs team is made up of purely university students that love what they are studying. To continue studying their passions, they need a job to pay for their education, rent, and other fun expenses that come along with pursuing what they love to do. Our employees take a lot of pride and care in fixing every phone, because it not only helps you, but it also helps them get one step closer to their dream job.
